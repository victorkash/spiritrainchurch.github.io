Welcome to Spirit Rain Church: A Beacon of Faith and Truth

Spirit Rain Church is a prayer and Word-based ministry dedicated to spreading the full Gospel with holiness as our watchword. Our purpose is to enlighten our congregation about key biblical events, including the Last Days, the Rapture, the Great Tribulation, the War of Armageddon, the Millennial Reign of Christ, the War of Gog and Magog, the Great White Throne Judgment, and the New Heaven and New Earth (Matt 24:14, 44; 1 Cor 15:51-52; Rev 20:7-15, 21:1-4).

We aim to proclaim the Word of Faith and the healing Gospel, fostering unity in the body of Christ and building believers to mature in the fullness of Christ (Eph 4:13). Through evangelistic outreaches, gospel campaigns, and media publications, we share messages of restoration, salvation, and victory, empowered by the Holy Spirit (Mark 16:15-20; Acts 2:1-17).

Our ministry emphasizes the inerrant and infallible Word of God, teaching the Trinity, water baptism by immersion, Holy Ghost baptism, and the laying on of hands for ministry and impartation (2 Tim 3:16-17; Col 2:9). Through our Spirit Rain Discipleship Programs, we train disciples to be fruitful and united in the truth. We also promote peaceful coexistence with others, respecting existing laws, and engage diverse groups with the Gospel of Jesus Christ (Heb 12:14; 1 Cor 9:19-23).
